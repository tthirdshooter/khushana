Meet the Artist

Hi, I'm Priya Malhotra

With over 5 years of professional experience in nail tech and luxury nail art, I treat every single nail like a mini canvas. I specialize in Japanese gel overlays, hand-painted intricate art, and healthy structured manicures.

My top priority is the health of your natural nails. I use only non-toxic, premium products to ensure your manicure looks stunning and lasts for weeks without chipping.
